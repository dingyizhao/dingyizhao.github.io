This line of work focuses on robust halo mass estimation for large galaxy group
catalogs, with an emphasis on reducing systematic bias and improving precision.

## Focus
- Feature design from group observables.
- Model comparison across tree-based and neural methods.
- Validation against simulations and observed scaling relations.

## Outcome
The framework supports more reliable halo-based studies of quenching,
environment, and mass assembly history.
