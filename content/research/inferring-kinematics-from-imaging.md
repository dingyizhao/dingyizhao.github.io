This direction develops computer-vision models that map imaging data to
resolved physical fields.

## Focus
- Generative modeling for kinematic reconstruction.
- End-to-end training with simulation-informed supervision.
- Evaluation of physical consistency and uncertainty.

## Outcome
The method aims to make kinematic information accessible at scale for surveys
without IFU-level spectroscopy.
