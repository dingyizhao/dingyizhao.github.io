This project investigates how halo-scale processes regulate star formation
shutdown in central galaxies.

## Focus
- Compare halo mass against stellar mass and structural indicators.
- Build controlled samples to separate confounding effects.
- Quantify quenching trends across mass and environment bins.

## Outcome
Results support the interpretation that halo mass plays a dominant role in
central-galaxy quenching.
