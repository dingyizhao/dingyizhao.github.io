## What It Does
- Learns image-to-physics mappings using diffusion models.
- Generates velocity and mass-distribution fields.
- Supports uncertainty-aware inference.

## Why It Matters
This project extends modern generative AI to an astrophysical inverse problem
with direct scientific utility.
