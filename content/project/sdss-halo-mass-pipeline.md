## What It Does
- Ingests SDSS group-level observables.
- Trains and evaluates halo-mass estimators.
- Produces calibrated predictions and diagnostics.

## Why It Matters
The pipeline provides a stable foundation for group catalog analysis and
downstream studies on quenching and environment.
