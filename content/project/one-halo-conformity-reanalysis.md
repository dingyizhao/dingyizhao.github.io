## What It Does
- Reconstructs conformity measurements with updated halo-mass estimates.
- Applies strict controls for mass and environment.
- Tests robustness across subsamples and model choices.

## Why It Matters
A cleaner measurement clarifies whether conformity reflects halo-driven
processes or sample-selection effects.
